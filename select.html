Over the course of the past five years, I’ve worked as a Magento ecommerce developer. My role involves communicating with clients to discuss site improvements, planning work, providing estimates, leading development teams on six week long sprints and manning the support board to assist with client’s urgent issues.

As well as my professional experience, I enjoy programming projects recreationally too. I like to try to program my own games using a variety of tools. Unity has been a recent favourite tool to use. I’m currently working on a project to port a childhood favourite game of mine, Dr. Robotnik’s Mean Bean Machine, to mobile.

My first experience with a programming language was Python. After acquiring a taste for web development, I enrolled in an online Python programming course ran by Rice University. The course challenged students to code their own games, starting with a simple tic-tac-toe simulator and finishing with a clone of the arcade classic 'Asteroids'.

I believe this helped my students to feel even more comfortable interacting in English.
